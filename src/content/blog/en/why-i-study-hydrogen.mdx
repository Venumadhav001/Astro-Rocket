1.5 years ago, I moved to Germany to study Hydrogen Technology. I have not shared much about this journey online, as I have focused on my work.

Now in my 4th semester and deep into my thesis — simulating a fuel cell and battery hybrid vehicle in MATLAB — I believe it is time to start sharing what I have been learning.

Hydrogen deserves more recognition in the clean mobility conversation. It is not just a fuel — it is an energy carrier. The more I study it, the more I believe it has a significant role in transport that many people may not fully realise.

This blog is where I share what I learn, one post at a time.

*Kalluri Devi Narasimha Venu Madhav — M.Sc. Hydrogen Technology, TH Rosenheim*
